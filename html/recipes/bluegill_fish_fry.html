Bluegill Fish Fry

Don't have a credit for this recipe, its been scratched on a scrap of paper in the cupboard for a couple decades and is great for cooking up fish.

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Servings: depends on your fishing skill

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoons salt

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients
  2. Wisk until smooth
  3. Heat oil to 365 fahrenheit
  4. Cook for 2 minutes on each side turning only once
  5. Place on paper towel and then wire rack (so the bottom doesn't get soggy)
